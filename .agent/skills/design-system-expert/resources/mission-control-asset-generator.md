# Mission Control Asset Generator

Official prompt library for maintaining material and lighting consistency across all GaborPortfolio assets.

## Core Directives
- **Format**: `webp` only.
- **Aspect Ratio**: Tiles are strictly **3:4 (Portrait)**. Backgrounds are **16:9 (Landscape)**.
- **Aesthetic**: "Futuristic Steampunk Elegant Dark Mode" (Copper, Gold, Carbon Fiber, Mahogany).

## Material Cheat Sheet
- **Copper**: `#b87333` (Polished/Weathered)
- **Gold**: `#d4af37` (Brushed/Burnished)
- **Base**: Carbon Fiber or Mahogany Wood.
- **Lighting**: Dark background, high-contrast focal points, warm golden or moody glow.

## Craftsman's Tip
Apply aggressive compression to `bg-mission-control.webp` as it is often partially obscured by HUD panels. Prioritize LCP by preloading the active cockpit image.
